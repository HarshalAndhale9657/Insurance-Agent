LIC New Jeevan Anand (Plan 915) - Participation Endowment Plan
Trusted Source: LIC of India

1. Overview:
This is one of the most popular plans in India. It offers a dual benefit: Protection (if you die) and Savings (if you survive).
Even after the policy matures and you get your money, the "Risk Cover" for death continues for the rest of your life. This is why it is called "Jeevan Anand" (Lifetime Joy).

2. Key Benefits:
- Death Benefit (During Policy Term): Sum Assured on Death + Vested Bonuses.
- Maturity Benefit (End of Term): Basic Sum Assured + Vested Bonuses.
- Lifetime Cover: Even after carrying the maturity amount, if the policyholder dies later (e.g., at age 80), the Nominee gets the Basic Sum Assured again.
