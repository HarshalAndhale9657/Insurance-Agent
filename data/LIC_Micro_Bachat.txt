LIC Micro Bachat (Plan 951) - Low Cost Savings Plan
Trusted Source: LIC of India

1. Overview:
A regular premium, non-linked, participating micro-insurance plan. It is designed specifically for low-income groups and rural households to provide a combination of protection and savings.

2. Key Benefits:
- Affordable: Premiums are very low.
- Auto Cover: If you miss a premium after paying for 3 years, the cover continues for 6 months automatically.
- Maturity Benefit: Sum Assured + Loyalty Additions (if any).
- Death Benefit: Sum Assured on Death (higher of 10 times annual premium or 7 times if age > 35) + Loyalty Additions.

- Minimum Age: 18 Years
- Maximum Age: 55 Years

4. Loan Facility:

5. Why it is good for Rural Families?
It works like a small savings account but gives you life insurance. It is perfect for small farmers or daily wage earners who want to save small amounts regularly.
