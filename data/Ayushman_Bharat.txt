Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY) - Health Insurance
Trusted Source: National Health Authority

1. Overview:
Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY is the world's largest government-funded health assurance scheme.
It provides health cover of Rs. 5 Lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.

2. Key Benefits:
- Cashless Access: No money needs to be paid by the beneficiary at the point of service (hospital).
- Portable: Beneficiaries can avail treatment in any empanelled public or private hospital across India.
- No Cap on Family Size: All members of eligible families are covered, regardless of age or gender.
- Pre-existing Diseases Covered: All pre-existing conditions are covered from day one.

3. Coverage Areas:
- Medical examination, treatment, and consultation
- Pre-hospitalization expenses
- Medicine and medical consumables
- Non-intensive and intensive care services
- Diagnostic and laboratory investigations
- Medical implantation services (where necessary)
- Accommodation benefits
- Food services
- Complications arising during treatment
- Post-hospitalization follow-up care up to 15 days

4. Eligibility:
- Based on the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011 data.
- Approx 10.74 crore poor and vulnerable entitled families (approx 50 crore beneficiaries).
- Households with no adult member between 16-59 years, female-headed households with no adult male member, SC/ST households, landless households deriving major income from manual casual labor.

5. Costs:
- Completely free for the beneficiary. The premium is shared by Central and State Governments (usually 60:40 ratio).
